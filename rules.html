Rules

Here are the rules of Texas Hold 'Em Poker:

  • Each player is dealt two private cards (known as 'hole cards').
  • Five community cards are dealt face-up on the 'board'.
  • Players use their hole cards and the community cards to make the best five-card hand.
  • The game consists of four betting rounds: Pre-Flop, Flop, Turn, and River.
  • Players can choose to Fold, Call, or Raise during their turn.
  • The computer acts as the dealer, managing the dealing of cards and the betting rounds.
  • The player with the best hand (or the last player remaining after all others have folded) wins the pot.
